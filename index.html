Krab Klashers

3D Online Multiplayer Arena Combat game made in a software engineering course at UCSC utilizing the SCRUM methodology. Blast through your friends in 4 different levels.

Developed in Unity3D with use of Photon Networking from ExitGames. As lead programmer of the team I learned quite a lot about using Unity 3D, making multiplayer games, and online networking, none of which I had worked with before.

  • Tool: Unity3D, Monodevelop, Photon, Scrum
  • Date: January 2015
